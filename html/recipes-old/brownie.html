Maybe not the most sensical recipe with chocolate, so much chia etc. but oh well it's yum

Ingredients

  • 125g Margerine
  • 125g Coconut oil
  • 200g Dark choc (Whittakers or some cooking chocs are vegan)
  • 1/2 C sugar
  • 4 Tbsp chia seeds ground
  • 2/3 C water
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 C plain flour or 140g gluten free flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 C cocoa

Method

  1. Mix ground chia and water leave aside to become gloopy
  2. Melt oils and choc together over low heat on stove
  3. Add dry ingredients and vanilla essence into a bowl
  4. Mix wet ingredients into dry
  5. Pour into baking tin. Bake at 180c for 50 min
  6. Let set preferably for more than four hours to become fudge like
