Stir Fry recipe from Jess. Have yet to try

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp rice wine or sherry
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp minced chilli
  • 1 Tbsp red curry paste
  • 2 spring onions
  • 2 Tbsp coriander leaf
Fry curry paste, then veges, add sauce towards end
