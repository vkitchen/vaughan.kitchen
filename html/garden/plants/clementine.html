Citrus clementine 'Corsica #2'

Extremely popular and arguably the best all round mandarin for mild areas. Compact and attractive grower with very heavy crops of good sized, highly coloured fruit with excellent apricot nectar like flavour.

  • Full sun. Well drained, fertile soil
  • Mild regions - will tolerate temps to around -3C
  • Water regularly during hot, dry spells
  • Regular fertilising from spring to mid-summer
  • 2m tall x 1.5m wide, after 8 years unpruned
  • Ripens winter, fruit will hang for months
  • Superb eating
  • Evergreen, suitable for container growing
