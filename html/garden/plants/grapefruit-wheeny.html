Citrus x paradisi

Australian Grapefruit

Not a true grapefruit but has great crops of large fruit with a sour, lemony flavour with no bitterness. Fruit will ripen in cooler areas

  • Full sun. Well drained, fertile soil
  • Mild regions - will tolerate temps to around -3C
  • Water regularly during hot, dry spells
  • Regular fertilising from spring to mid-summer
  • 2m tall x 2m wide after 8 years unprunned
  • Fruits late winter to spring. Later in cooler areas
  • Yummy for breakfast, juicing & marmalade
  • Evergreen, suitable for container growing
