Gnoms > Recipes > Chickpea Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 2 C cooked drained chickpeas
  • 1/3 C orange juice
  • 4 lg eggs
  • 1 C packed brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2/3 C cocoa
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • zest of 1 orange (optional
  • 1 C choc chips (optional)
  • 1 C toasted hazelnuts (optional)

Method

  1. Process chickpeas & orange juice until very smooth
  2. Add eggs one at a time pulsing after each addition
  3. In a med bowl whisk together sugar, sifted cocoa, baking powder & baking soda
  4. Add to processer and pulse until just blended
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake approx 50 mins
