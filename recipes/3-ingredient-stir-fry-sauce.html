About

This very simple stir fry sauce works both as a marinade or a quick meal when put over veges. It can be served with rice or vermicilli noodles. If using as a marinade it can be especially nice when coated and fried. For a simple coating use sesame seeds mixed into corn starch.

Ingredients

  • 2 parts soy sauce
  • 2 parts sweet chille sauce
  • 1 part sesame oil
Shake to combine
