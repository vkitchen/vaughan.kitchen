About

Stir Fry recipe from Jess. Very nice

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 tsp crushed garlic
  • 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 4 tsp brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vege stock powder
  • 2 tsp cornflour
Shake to combine
