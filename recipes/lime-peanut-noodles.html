About

Stir Fry recipe from Nadia

Ingredients

  • 16 oz tofu or 1 ½ cups of seitan cut into cubes or strips.
  • ¼ cup soy sauce or tamari
  • 3 tbs peanut butter
  • 2 ½ tbs lime juice
  • ½ tsp chili flakes
  • ¼ tsp ginger powder
  • 1 to 2 tsp of oil
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • 2 cups of chopped carrots
  • 1 can bamboo shoots
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 package wide flat noodles or 2 cups of jasmine rice

Instructions

  1. Make peanut sauce by whisking together soy sauce, peanut butter, lime juice, chili, and ginger until combines and set aside.
  2. Cook noodles until just softened, then drain and rise with cold water or make rice and set aside.
  3. Cut tofu in cubes or strips and brown in frying pan.
  4. To prepare the kale, carrots, bamboo shoots, heat oil in wok or frying pan, add the carrots, bamboo shoots and kale and stir periodically.
  5. Combine tofu or seitan and the peanut sauce into the wok, stir until all vegetables are cooked.
  6. Serve over rice or noodle
