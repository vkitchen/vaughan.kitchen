About

This very simple baked bean recipe will get you there my friend.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 C Great Northern Beans soaked overnight and then cooked
  • 3 Cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion
  • 3 or so cloves
  • couple sprigs thyme
  • few sage leaves
  • salt to taste
  • sugar to taste
Fry onions until soft and slightly browned. Add tomatoes and spices/herbs (in a spice bag), simmer for a couple hours until soft and mostly dissolved. Add beans and cook for 30 minutes or so. Add sugar until not so sour. Add salt to taste.
