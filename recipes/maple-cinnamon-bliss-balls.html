Home > Recipes > Maple Cinnamon Bliss Balls

Ingredients

  • 2 C almonds, coursely ground
  • 1/2 C maple syrup
  • 2 Tbsp cinnamon
  • 2 Tbsp coconut oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 C sultanas (optional)

Method

  1. Mix ingredients in large bowl
  2. Form into balls
  3. Fridge for 30 mins to firm up
