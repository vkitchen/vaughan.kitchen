Cavolo Nero / Tuscan Kale

Chosen for consistent performance in the home garden. As versatile as it is tasty, Tuscan Kale is a leafy vegetable high in vitamins and fibre. A popular ingredient in Italian dishes, this Kale produces upright crinkly leaves that are deep green to blue in colour.

AFTER HARVEST USE: A hearty addition to soups, stews and casserole dishes. Chop and freeze any surplus leaves for later use.

  • POSITION: Full sun
  • PLANT: 40cm apart
  • MATURITY: 8-10 weeks
