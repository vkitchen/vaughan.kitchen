Gnoms > Garden > Plants > Lemon Verbena

Info From Tag

Add to fruit puddings, apple jelly and homemade ice-cream. Also makes a refreshing tea.

Growing tips: Prefers shelter from the sun and light, well drained soil. Can be grown indoors in a cool place all year and placed outdoors in summer.

  • Full Sun to Part Shade
  • 3m x 3m
  • Spacing 60cm
  • Culinary Use
